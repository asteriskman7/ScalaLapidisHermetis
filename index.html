Scala Lapidis Hermetis
(et casus)
Alchemist, I need your help to save the world!

I need you to generate alchemical energy so I can push away the moon and avoid an endless night. Unfortunately, you are not wise enough to do the job very quickly. However, by creating Philosopher's Stones you will become much more powerful.

I've provided you a Scala Lapidis Hermetis (Ladder of the Philosopher's Stone) grid to help you out but be aware that you will encounter casus along the way that will drop you back down. Creating lower level elements along the way will invoke more scalae and remove casus.

Luckily, this whole process is so simple that even a one headed human like you will be able to do it with your eyes closed so you don't need to take any actions beyond observing. Just watch the crucible follow the alembic.

Good luck!
